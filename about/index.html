About Me

Yaman Parasher is currently a recipient of the prestigious Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree in Photonic Integrated Circuits, Sensors & Networks (PIXNET), since 2019. As a part of this degree program, he had already completed his first two semesters in TeCIP Institute, Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, Pisa, Italy, and currently attending his third semester at Aston University, Birmingham, United Kingdom, before going to Osaka University, Japan in the fourth semester to complete his master's thesis. He received Integrated B.Tech (Electronics & Communication Engineering) + M.Tech (Wireless Communication & Networks) from the School of ICT, Gautam Buddha University in May 2018. Where apart from his regular academic projects he had done various internships related to embedded systems, telecommunication & hardware design.

 

Experience encompasses working on various in lab optical system design software tools & workbench along with expertise in modeling, design, & simulation of advanced photonic integrated circuits. Published research papers in reputed Science Cited Indexed (SCI) journals such as Elsevier, Springer, and international conference of Optical Society of America (OSA), LAOP-2018 in Peru.

 

His research interests include Integrated Optics (Photonic Integrated Circuits), Advanced & Hybrid Optical Communication Systems, Free Space Optics, Spatial Division Multiplexing, Ultradense Wavelength Division Multiplexing, Other Research Interest includes Machine Learning, Big Data, Embedded Systems, IoT.

  • Layout Design Tools - KLayout, Mentor Graphics L-Edit, HSPICE.

  • Programming languages - Python, R, C/C++, TCL/TK, PHP, mySQL.
  • Frameworks - Git, Hadoop, CodeIgniter, Laravel , LATEX
 

Besides his research, he has been active in Enactus Aston as a Market Research Manager, where his main task includes Managing promotion, positioning, creating & employing innovative market strategies for Enactus Aston's future endeavors.
